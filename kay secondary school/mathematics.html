About our department

Mathematics department is a very vibrant department in kay secondary school. We have made the all students aware that Mathematics is in the centre of all the careers and therefore they have to do it with all the enthusiasm and positive attitude. The perfomrance of Mathematics in kay secondary school is above average. The teachers are enthusiastic and have created a good rapport with the students. The school's perfomrance in Mathematics is exceptional in the Kenya Certificate of Secondary Education national exams. kay secondary school is a centre for the Brookside national mathlete (Mathematics Contest) sponsored by Brookside dairies and Strathmore University where the winners are given scholarships and other awards.
